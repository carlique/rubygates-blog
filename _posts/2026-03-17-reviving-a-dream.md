# Reviving a Dream: The Rubygates Experiment

Back in 2008, we embarked on an ambitious project: a massive multiplayer cooperative game, 
built with the web technologies of the time. The project shut down around 2010, but I never deleted it. 
It sat, zipped on my drive, waiting for its moment.

That moment came recently, almost by accident. 
As someone who now pushes the boundaries of AI in my day job, I wondered: could today’s AI revive and even build this game with minimal direct coding from me?

Now, I act as the stakeholder, guiding AI agents—Claude, Codex, and others—to handle the coding. 
I’m learning along the way, testing the limits of AI-driven development. 
This blog will chronicle my journey—reviving a fantasy world, a 2008 dream, and seeing just how far AI can take us. 

Let’s explore what’s possible.
